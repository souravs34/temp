Computer Society of India

Computer Society of India is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals It has 72 chapters across India, 511 student branches, and 100,000 members

PICT CSI Student Branch

PCSB brings together the technical and non technical education, number of seminars from experts. PCSB not only support the professional growth but also give you the opportunity for life-long learning and career development. PCSB is a platform for recognition of technical excellence.

We encourage the student member to organize the events which imbibes the skills of management, self-confidence and helps to exchange views and information, learn and share ideas. We believe that every person on this planet is blessed with some or the other quality and is dexterous at something and exploring the acme within every individual is the aim of PCSB.

Membership Benefits

  • Take part in various CSI events on a National Level.
  • Attend the Annual Council Meeting of International Federation for Information Processing (IFIP).
  • Inspire the next generation through the international coding competition organized by SEARCC.
  • Share the best approaches to technology policy, strategy and implementation through Hackathons and other Global events.
  • Have MOUs with Project Management Institute(PMI) and other innovation bodies.
  • To tackle the problems in Technology and improve the problem solving skills, creativity and technical skill sets
